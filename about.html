Chi siamo

Bicego Damiano

I am Bicego Damiano, the creator of Nexter. Nexter is an open source project that I started in 2026 with the goal of creating a simple and intuitive programming language. I have a background in computer science and a passion for coding, which led me to develop Nexter as a way to make programming more accessible to everyone. I am committed to maintaining and improving Nexter, and I welcome contributions from the community to help it grow and evolve.
